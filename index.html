Welcome

I'm Derek, and I'm spending the summer working in Prof. Stephen Becker's Information Extraction Lab at the University of Colorado Boulder. In October, I will begin PhD studies in Mathematical Analysis at the University of Cambridge, as a member of the Cambridge Centre for Analysis and the Cantab Capital Institute for the Mathematics of Information. My current research investigates problems in optimization, machine learning, signal/image processing, and the role of algebraic geometry in each of these contexts.
